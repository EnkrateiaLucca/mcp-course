# A Shakespearean Sonnet: The Model Context Protocol

When models seek to commune with the world,
A protocol doth bridge the vast divide,
Through standardized paths, connections unfurled,
Where context flows like an unending tide.

The servers offer tools and data bright,
While clients make their humble, reasoned plea,
In JSON's structure, clear and light,
Two parties dance in perfect harmony.

No longer bound by rigid, siloed walls,
The AI now can reach beyond its ken,
It answers to the user's earnest calls,
With access vast, unknown to mortal men.

So praise the protocol that sets minds free,
To augment human capability.
